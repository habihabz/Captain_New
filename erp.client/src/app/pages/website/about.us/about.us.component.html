HUSI International

A trailblazer in the sports industry, empowering athletes to lead and win.

HUSI International, established in 2012 in Kerala by a dynamic and visionary team, recognized the immense potential of the sports goods market and embarked on a journey to craft premium-quality sporting products that elevate performance and experience for athletes and enthusiasts alike.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our commitment to quality is reflected in our state-of-the-art factories located in Kerala and Punjab, where raw materials are transformed into high-performance sports gear. These facilities are supported by advanced processing units in Tamil Nadu and Gujarat, ensuring superior durability, consistency, and innovation across every product.

Vision & Mission

Driven by a resolute vision to inspire and nurture sportsmanship, HUSI International is committed to crafting high-quality products that cater to a diverse range of sports enthusiasts.

Our mission is to foster excellence, promote health and well-being, and support victory in every facet of sports.

Lead by Purpose

Innovation, performance, and athlete-centric design guide everything we create.

Global Market Presence

Captain’s influence extends across India, neighboring countries, and the GCC region. Trusted by over a thousand dealers, clubs, academies, and professional athletes, our commitment to quality has built a loyal and growing community.
